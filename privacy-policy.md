# Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal or sensitive user data.

The extension operates locally within the user's browser and is used only to inspect NetSuite record fields, internal IDs, and page structure for development and administration purposes.

No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or analytics platforms.

The extension does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or remote data storage.

If this policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page.
